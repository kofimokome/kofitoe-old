How To Play

Each player has 3 'seeds' . Place your 'seed' on the board by clicking an empty location. After the second player plays his/her third 'seed', you can move you 'seed' from one location to another by clicking on the the 'seed' you want to move, then click the new location.

For Online players, you can:

  • Create a new game: You can create a new game by filling your name and the session password. You will be given a session name and password. You can then give the session name and password to your friend.
  • Join a game: You can join a game by filling the session name and password. You will be asked to enter your name.
  • Note: Only two players are allowed in a session ththeeh d.
